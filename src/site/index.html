#NoTouchChallenge
Built by Vedran to help (gently) encourage everyone to avoid touching their face and help my coworkers at Noom stay healthy throughout the COVID-19 crisis.
We all know the CDC recommends not touching our faces to stop the spread of COVID-19


When you are using your laptop, #NoTouchChallenge monitors how long you can go without touching your face. Share your best time on Twitter and Facebook to help spread the healthy behavior.


I built this web app to help (gently) encourage everyone to avoid touching their face and help my coworkers at Noom stay healthy throughout the COVID-19 crisis.


We aren't storing or transmitting any faces, images, or videos and won't be selling or profiting from this. We are doing this to help everyone stay safe during this COVID-19 epidemic.

Code on Github. Powered by face-api.js and handtrack.js.
