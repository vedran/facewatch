#NoTouchChallenge
Built by Vedran to share with coworkers at Noom as we work from home together.
The CDC recommends not touching your face to stop the spread of COVID-19.

Take the #NoTouchChallenge and see how long you can last.


This page runs best in fullscreen on a laptop or desktop with a webcam.

No faces, images, or videos are ever transmitted to any remote server. All processing is done in your own browser.

Code on Github. Powered by face-api.js and handtrack.js.
